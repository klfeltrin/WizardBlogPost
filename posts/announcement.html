Announcement

Saturday 25 April 2026

Dear Wizard friends, I’m here to announce the end of my journey at this school. It’s a strange feeling, but after four years of learning English, it’s time to say goodbye. I can remember all the new people I’ve met, including students, teachers and all the professionals responsible for making this possible.

I’m gonna miss all the lessons i’ve learned, all the conversations about those lessons and the moments of happiness, talking about our lives or the things that we like. We’ve made a rich exchange of knowledge, by sharing facts, movies and books that we like, or just telling to each other special moments of our lives.

It wasn’t always easy, because it required a lot of discipline from me to balance my personal, professional and educational life. At the same time, when I was listening to the lessons, or talking to my teacher, it was like all my problems were gone. Those conversations were the highlights of my weeks, and I was always excited, waiting for the moment to talk about anything and listen to new stories. Believe me, those moments were super special for me.

But I need to confess, it’s hard to move on after so long, and it’s not easy to say goodbye to something that changed my life. But it’s necessary, and what calms me down is the fact that I see myself as a better version of me than when I started the course, and I can also see these improvements on the people that stayed with me all this time.

I need to say a special thanks to my teachers, which made this journey easier, and helped me not just on the learning of a second language, but teaching me how to believe in myself and see the world from a different perspective. If you’re reading this… thank you so much, I’m always grateful for that and I’ll never forget you.

With all of that said, it’s time to forge new paths. I’m gonna use all the knowledge that I acquired to find new opportunities, but always remembering those moments I’ve passed through to get there. Thank you Wizard, for the bonds created and for all the moments that I will remember forever.
